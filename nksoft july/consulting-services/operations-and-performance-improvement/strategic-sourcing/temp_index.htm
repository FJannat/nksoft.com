Strategic Sourcing

How much value are you realizing from external spend?  Over the last 15 years this area has evolved from an “off radar” nuisance to a valued source of competitive advantage.

Third party expenses generally represent 30% - 70% of total company expenses.  Among the many reasons pursue Strategic Sourcing are to:

  • Significantly improve cost structure without reducing headcount;
  • To reduce operational risk;
  • Improve quality, service levels, and cycle times;
  • Improve cash flow;
  • Improve integration within and across business and operating units

Strategic Sourcing has been a core offering of NKSoft since the beginning.  We are experienced, expert sourcing professionals focused on identifying and delivering short-term sourcing value and building Strategic Sourcing capabilities needed for sustaining and increasing the value of your external spend.  We bring a library of benchmarks and client-tested best practice tools and approaches to:

  • Develop and execute a self-funding Strategic Sourcing plan
  • Evaluate spend and associated Strategic Sourcing priorities  
  • Define supply markets and identify qualified suppliers
  • Define business, operations, and technical requirements
  • Accelerate competitive sourcing efforts (traditional, one-on-one, online)
  • Ensure structured, aggressive negotiations
  • Integrate legal and business performance requirements as part of the RFX process to streamline contracting, reduce vendor transition time frames, address business risk, and ensure continuous improvement
