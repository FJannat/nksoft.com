Meet Our Principals

Our Principals have substantial consulting experience and have spent significant time leading multiple client engagements across industries. They also bring prior consulting experience from blue-chip management consultancies. These are the future leaders of our firm.

NKSoft Principals have spent significant time leading multiple client engagements and across industries. They also bring prior consulting experience from firms such as McKinsey & Co., A.T. Kearney,  Accenture and others.

Functional Expertise: Strategy & Execution, Business Planning, M&A Support, Sales & Marketing Management, Marketing Resource Management

Industry Expertise: Energy, Banking & Asset Management

Industry Expertise: Energy, Private Equity
