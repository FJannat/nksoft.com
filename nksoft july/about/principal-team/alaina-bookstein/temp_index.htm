Principal Team

Alaina Bookstein, Principal

Email | 312.357.6740

Functional Expertise: Strategy & Execution, Business Planning, M&A Support, Sales & Marketing Management, Marketing Resource Management

Industry Expertise: Energy, Banking & Asset Management

With work experience in Europe, Asia, and North America Alaina has 15 years of experience in strategy development and execution in energy and financial services.

Alaina joined NKSoft in 2006 and has extensive experience in strategy development. With a specialty in the energy industry, Alaina has worked with leading utilities, merchant generators, retail electric providers and energy solutions companies to create customer and shareholder value. Her functional experience has focused on corporate and business unit strategy, profitable growth, M&A, partnerships, and marketing management.

Prior to joining NKSoft, Alaina was a consultant with McKinsey & Company and worked in senior marketing and sales positions at American Express and UBS.
