Industry Director Team

Meet Our Industry Directors

NKSoft Industry Directors have spent significant time in specific industries of interest, bringing significant industry experience at Fortune 500 firms. Feel free to peruse the experience snapshots of our Industry Directors. If you would like to learn more about our capabilities you may contact them directly.

Pankaj K. Garg, Industry Director

In early 1990s, PK helped General Motors bring the first commercial electric vehicle ‘EV1” to the market.   Today, PK brings extensive global experience to NKSoft client executives in energy, industrial and automotive sectors.

Functional Expertise: Strategy & Execution, Business Planning, M&A Support, Operations & Strategic IT, Outsourcing, Offshoring, Strategic Sourcing, Supply Chain

Industry Expertise: Commercial & Industrial, Consumer & Retail, Energy

John McNeel, Industry Director

John has more than 30 years of energy consulting experience across generation and distribution, including strategic planning, marketing and sales, performance improvement, asset management, and systems.

Functional Expertise: Strategy & Execution, Profitable Growth, Business Planning, M&A Support, Sales & Marketing Management

Industry Expertise: Energy

Ernie Csiszar, Senior Advisor

Ernie was Director of Insurance for the State of South Carolina and president of the NAIC.   He serves as a Senior Advisor to NKSoft Group's health and insurance practices.

Functional Expertise: Strategy & Execution, Profitable Growth, Business Planning, M&A Support, Sales & Marketing Management, Channel Management, Sales Force Effectiveness, Insurance, Healthcare

Industry Expertise: Insurance, Healthcare
