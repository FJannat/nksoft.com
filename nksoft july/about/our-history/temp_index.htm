Our History

Founded by Hands-On, Seasoned Consultants

NKSoft Group was founded in 1998 by a group of experienced business consultants with a passion for working closely with clients to improve business performance, bridging the gap between the Strategy and Execution.

NKSoft is a firm built on the vision and passion of a group of seasoned practitioners, all of whom have spent significant time at leading global management consulting firms, such as McKinsey & Co., The Boston Consulting Group, Booz & Company, A.T. Kearney, Accenture and others. Their desire was to create a consulting practice where they could remain personally involved in project work throughout their careers, and provide clients focused senior advice across a broad range of management issues.

Over the years, NKSoft has delivered a unique proposition in a crowded consulting marketplace. We structure our work and relationships based on how the client wants to be served. We do not impose one-size-fits-all methodologies or cookie-cutter approaches on projects, we provide experience-based, tailored approaches to specific client needs. We recognize the influence our advice can have both on the companies and the careers of the individuals who engage us, and we take this responsibility very seriously. In simple terms, our objective is to help our clients achieve superior, sustainable and profitable growth. In 2001, in recognition of our success, NKSoft was identified by Consulting Magazine as one of the Seven Jewels of Consulting.

NKSoft's organic growth has outpaced the industry's growth rates since our inception. We have grown through selectively recruiting experienced consultants from top-tier firms as well as leaders from industry who share our passion for creative and effective problem-solving and delivering results.

In 2008, NKSoft began a new chapter in its life: we joined the Mahindra Satyam family. Mahindra Satyam is a leading global Consulting, IT and Business services company. NKSoft has retained its management consulting independence and objectivity, and operates with a high degree of autonomy. But now, NKSoft also benefits from the broader capabilities of Mahindra Satyam, as well as those of all Mahindra companies, as clients increasingly seek more integrated consulting, business and IT services.
