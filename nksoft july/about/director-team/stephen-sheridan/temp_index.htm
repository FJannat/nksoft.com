Stephen Sheridan is a founding member and Chief Executive Officer of NKSoft Group. Steve has over 25 years experience consulting Fortune 1000 clients in sectors including energy and technology. His functional focus includes business strategy, M&A and organization design. He has expertise in and has developed intellectual capital on sales and marketing effectiveness, particularly related to clients shifting from a product-centric business model to a solutions-centric model. His work has been published in The Journal of Business Strategy, the Handbook of Business Strategy and he has contributed to published articles in Fortune and Business Week on the Horizontal Organization.

Prior to NKSoft, Steve was Divisional President for Renaissance Worldwide and a Senior Manager for McKinsey & Company.
