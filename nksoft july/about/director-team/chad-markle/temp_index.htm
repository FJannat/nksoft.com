Director Team

Chad Markle, Director

Email | 312.357.6740

Functional Expertise: Strategy & Execution, Business Planning, M&A Support, Operations & Strategic IT, Operations Architecture, Outsourcing, Offshoring, Shared Services Design, Strategic Sourcing, Supply Chain, IT Planning and Management

Industry Expertise: Banking & Asset Management, Energy, Healthcare, Commercial & Industrial, Insurance, Consumer & Retail, Technology & Telecom

Chad, as leader of NKSoft's Strategic IT and Operations practice, helps CEOs and other senior executives solve their most pressing business issues, not simply prepare plans. His focus includes restructuring programs, applications of strategic technology, and harnessing growth opportunities.

He has worked across number of industries including: Energy, Utilities and Clean Tech; Distribution and Wholesale; Technology Services; Insurance (P&C, Health, Life); High Tech and Internet; Financial Services - wealth management, institutional securities, and investment management; Consumer Packaged Goods and Real Estate and related services.

Chad works with business and technology executives on a full range of issues and has particular expertise with maximizing business impact of Information Technology. Recent client experience includes leading a global beverage manufacturer through an information analytics strategy, leading an IT transformation and outsourcing effort for a provider of annuities, driving online search innovation for a global distributor, overseeing the supply chain transformation of a global wholesaler, and designing a competitively-differentiated business model for a large retail energy provider.

Earlier in his career, Chad served as Vice President of Mergers and Integration at Move.com, Vice President of Operations and Technology at IPIX Corporation, Manager at A. T. Kearney and Project Leader at Morgan Stanley & Co.

Education

  • The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania - BS in economics, strategic management and finance
  • Columbia University - Certificate in Technical Architecture and Object-Oriented Design

Personal Interests

Wakeboarding, gaming, Americana music, family time
