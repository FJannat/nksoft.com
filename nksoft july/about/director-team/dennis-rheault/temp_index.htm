Dennis Rheault, Director

Email | 312.357.6740

Functional Expertise: Strategy & Execution, Customer Insights, Profitable Growth, Business Planning, M&A Support, New Market Entry, Sales & Marketing Management, Brand Experience, Solutions Creation, Channel Management, Marketing Resource Management, Sales Force Effectiveness, Pricing Strategy, Portfolio Complexity

Industry Expertise: Commercial & Industrial,Consumer & Retail, Technology & Telecom, Private Equity, Healthcare

Dennis has spent over 25 years consulting for technology and industrial products clients.  Prior to joining NKSoft, Dennis was a Vice President at Motorola for corporate strategy and development and a partner at the Boston Consulting Group.

Dennis Rheault has been at NKSoft since 2000 and leads the high tech and industrial practices. In total, Dennis has over 25 years experience consulting for technology and industrial products clients. His areas of focus include strategy development and implementation, organization effectiveness and design, process redesign, and mergers and acquisitions (valuation through post-merger integration).  Dennis has published articles on a range of topics including: the role of the corporate center, effective strategy development processes, multi-channel strategies, and the evolution of channels.

Prior to NKSoft, Dennis was a Vice President at Motorola, responsible for corporate strategy and development. He also spent 15 years consulting with the Boston Consulting Group (BCG).
