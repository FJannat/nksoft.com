Director Team

Stuart Gunn, Director

Email | 312.357.6740

Functional Expertise: Strategy & Execution, Customer & Market Insights, Profitable Growth, Business Planning, Sales & Marketing Management, Channel Management, Pricing Strategy, Portfolio Complexity, Operations Architecture, Outsourcing, Shared Services Design, Strategic Sourcing

Industry Expertise: Banking & Asset Management, Healthcare, Insurance

Stuart is a leader of NKSoft’s healthcare and financial services practices, with 25 years of consulting and industry experience.

Stuart joined NKSoft in 2005 after serving as the Group SVP of Strategy, Acquisitions, and Corporate Finance at ABN AMRO, and a Vice President at A.T. Kearney.  He is passionate about helping organizations develop and implement strategies to navigate disruptive periods.  Of late, Stuart has helped health payers understand and respond to an evolving legislative, regulatory and competitive environment.

Education

  • MBA, with high honors, from the University of Chicago
  • AB in economics, magna cum laude, from Princeton University

Personal Interests

Spending time with his three children, golf, running, Blackhawks and Cubs
