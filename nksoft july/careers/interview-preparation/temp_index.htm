Interview Prep

Practice Makes Perfect

Case interviews are an integral part of the recruiting process in any management consulting firm, and are used at NKSoft to test some of the basic skills we believe are necessary for anyone to succeed in a career in Consulting.

Therefore, case interviews are an excellent opportunity for candidates to demonstrate their problem solving, analytical and communication skills, applied to a hypothetical business issue or dilemma.

In preparing for case interviews, there is no substitute for practicing. Practicing cases, alone or in groups, will make you more comfortable with the case interview process, will help you become more familiar with management frameworks and their application, and will build up your confidence for when your interview day comes.
