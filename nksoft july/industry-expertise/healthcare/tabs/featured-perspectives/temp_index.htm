Healthcare

  • Expertise
  • Featured Perspectives
  • In the Media

The current environment for health insurers is challenging, to say the least. Most payers are actively responding to health care legislation and regulations. However, resulting changes in market mix, increasing provider concentration, evolving sources of competitive advantage, and even potential fundamental shifts in health care financing mechanisms have created an uncertain strategic and operating environment. Strategies and tactics developed over the next few years will determine winners and losers in the health insurance industry. NKSoft professionals, with deep experience in the market, are here to help.

While we offer a full range of services, the focus of our practice includes:

  • Strategy development and response to health care legislation (PPACA)
  • Service and operations design & improvement
  • Distribution strategy & producer compensation design
  • Commercial group market strategy development and implementation
  • Strategic sourcing and procurement optimization
  • Developing technology-enabled business strategies
