Management Team

iMeet SmarterUtility

 

NKSoft management team includes award winning industry veterans from Utility industry, IBM, Ernst & Young and Price Waterhouse. The representative management team members are as follows:



Aubrey Roberts III
Managing Director
Aubrey L. Roberts III is a business and management professional with a twenty-seven year track record of successfully helping companies improve their business operations and increase profits. Mr. Roberts has improved the operations of several important companies such as Southwest Airlines, American Airlines, CompUSA, and Vanstar to name a few.  
Mr. Roberts, a native Texan, moved to Dallas after graduating from the University of Texas at Austin in 1983 to begin working in the Information Technology department of Vantage Companies Incorporated, a real estate investment and development company. After four years in information technology, Aubrey was invited to join the Management Consulting practice of Ernst & Young.
Aubrey is also dedicated to responding to areas of community need and enjoys working, when he can, with local non-profits (Meals-on-Wheels & Globe Aware). Mr. Roberts continues to bring his considerable talents and his outstanding consulting and business operations expertise to bear in his role as Managing Director at NKSoft Corporation.

 
Nizam Uddin,
Managing Director



Farooq Sobhan,
Director
Farooq Sobhan a former Bangladeshi diplomat and civil society leader served in various capacities in the Bangladesh government and foreign service. He was the Ambassador of Bangladesh to the People's Republic of China (1987–1990) and High Commissioner to the Federation of Malaysia (1984–1987) and the Republic of India (1992–1995). Besides, he was the Foreign Secretary of Bangladesh (1995–1997). He also served as the Special Envoy of the Caretaker Government, with the rank and status of a Cabinet Minister. Since 2000, he is serving as President of Bangladesh Enterprise Institute (BEI), an independent non-profit research organization focusing on private sector development in Bangladesh.



Jim Volpe,
Director
Jim Volpe, a Certified Management Consultant, has accomplished business development professional, Managing Director level in Big-6 firms (Arthur Andersen and Price Waterhouse) and top-tier product/software companies (Cisco and Oracle). His experience of over 20 year sales and delivery career reflects strong track-record of identifying and cultivating business relationships generating highly profitable multi-million dollar sales product and services. In addition, more than 15 years he was a part of the Energy & Utilities industry as a Managing Director and SME with an extensive background in assessments, architecture and deployment of smarter industry solutions such as Meter Data Management Systems and Energy Efficiency Systems. He has demonstrated the ability to leverage extensive business and systems background in order to help customers embrace a vision, taking it through to an implemented solution and become a reference account.
