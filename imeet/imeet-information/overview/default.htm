iMeet

Bringing people into one circle from all around the world.....

iMeet puts the emphasis on collaborative communication, a communication system where a group of individuals have open communication, share common thoughts, ideas, or beliefs, and are working towards a common goal. It combines both the exchange of information and the opportunity to strengthen positive relational dynamics between the individuals who are communicating.

 iMeet allows groups to achieve the following things:

  • Share a common goal or set of goals that lead to results-oriented collaborative efforts

  • Show mutual respect, consideration and understanding for each other
  • Assure proper project management and knowledge management support
  • Engage in enterprise social networking to accommodate the natural tendencies of interaction

iMeet Features

To bring working groups together collaboratively, iMeet has been built with the following features:

  • iMeet Dashboard provides a real-time snapshot of progress as well serving as the command and control center for users daily schedules, assignments, tasks, connections, issues, etc.
  • Knowledge Management delivers the capabilities to share knowledge, opinion and insight as the knowledge base is expanded, evolved, and published which creates opportunities to finish work faster and more efficiently within the collaborative environment.
  • iMeet Mini to deliver an integrated audio/video collaborative experience
  • Project Management to handle the collaborative work flow of teams
  • Social Networking to accommodate group interactions and strengthen company cultural bonds
