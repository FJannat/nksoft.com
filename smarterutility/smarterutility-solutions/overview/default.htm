Single Source for the Utility Transfromation Information

 

SmartUtilities represent an unprecedented and permanent change in the way suppliers of power, gas and water will produce, transport, store and deliver energy to households. While gas and water services providers will witness more of an evolution in their sector, the power industry is facing a real revolution as a result of new regulations and changes in demand.

This revolution will see the introduction of Smart Meters and Smart Grids leading to tremendous investment and development in Information Technology to create connected systems that can communicate with one another. It is a revolution that companies in the sector cannot afford to ignore.

SmarterUtility Features

As the practical and useful source of relevant information on Utilities Transformation, SmarterUtility offers collaborative information on different sections (Smart Grid, Smart Home, Smart Meter, Distributed Automation, Telecommunication, etc.) with various categories (News, Regulatory Filings, Business Cases, Use Cases, Architectures, Requirements, etc.).
