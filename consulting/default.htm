NKSoft Consulting Profile

NKSoft Consulting is an innovative e-Commerce solution and consulting company with 56 employees throughout the U.S. The company offers its ECRM solution, tools and application products, along with related consulting, education, and support services.

NKSoft Consulting Services

NKSoft Consulting Services, the professional services and consulting division of NetKnolwedge Technologies Corporation, can help you maximize your investment in information technology.

Along with its U.S consulting operations, NKSoft Consulting Services employs 200 consultants internationally in 3 countries, we have the resources to deliver multi-site, enterprise-wide solutions.

Available services include technology, industry, business, and functional knowledge, along with state-of-the-art management and development tools, and structured methods, to help maximize your investment in technology. We have technical experience in software architecture, hardware, system performance and capacity planning, as well as functional knowledge in accounting, finance, distribution, manufacturing, and human resources. Additionally, we can access a knowledge bank of proven practices and consulting solutions from successful client engagements around the world.

NKSoft Consulting Services offers the experience to provide industry-leading consulting services for open source systems and client/server environments. Its specialized services encompass all aspects of technology implementation, including Custom Development, Application Implementation, Advanced Technologies, Business Consulting Services, Change Management, Core Technologies, and Program Management.

Engagements are delivered using NKSoft Method, our structured, full life cycle, complete solution framework and approach. Our method provides proven practices for solving the business, technology, and organizational challenges faced by our clients. Continually refined and updated, based on NKSoft’s experience on successful projects worldwide, NKSoft Method can be applied as a framework to help develop solutions to solve your business challenges.

Technological advances are transforming the way businesses operate. To be a leader in this changing environment, you need to team with today’s advanced technology experts who apply extensive industry expertise to deliver solutions around the globe. NKSoft Consulting Services can assemble a team with the knowledge and skills necessary to quickly deliver the comprehensive business solutions you require. Our commitment to your success requires that we help you get the most out of your NKSoft products. That is why, through NKSoft Consulting Services, we provide the dedicated support, the consulting and industry expertise, and the comprehensive education you need to complete your NKSoft solution.

NKSoft offers a broad range of consulting services—from Business Process Re-engineering (BPR) to custom-development and application implementation. Through our technology transfer services, NKSoft Consulting Services can help you gain the knowledge you need to fully leverage your information resources, and effectively plan for your future information management needs.

NKSoft Business Consulting Services

NKSoft Business Consulting Services provides solutions to maximize your investment in NKSoft’s products and services. NKSoft consultants design processes and systems to streamline your corporate infrastructure, which can result in dramatic reductions in cost and cycle time, as well as improved responsiveness and customer satisfaction.

Each NKSoft engagement employs methods that provide a structured approach to analysis, visualization, and redesign of business processes. We use a set of proven re-engineering templates, called Dominant Designs, which have worked for many leading companies. Dominant Designs help us provide a vision of the re-engineered company. We then work closely with you to design and implement superior business processes to achieve that vision. Our method and process modeling repository tools reduce design time and coordinate integration with technology implementation efforts.

NKSoft’s Business Consulting Services include the following:

  • Enterprise Business Process Re-engineering (BPR): BPR captures major improvement opportunities that support the strategic goals of the enterprise. We operationalize these opportunities with more effective and flexible processes and systems.
  • Center for Corporate Information Services Excellence (CIO): The CIO practice draws on various NKSoft products and services to provide guidance in Information System (IS) Strategy, IS effectiveness, and system development. This service is delivered in conjunction with Change Management and custom education, Advanced Technologies, Application Implementation Services and Custom-Development tools and services.
